About Biological Molecules Lab

This interactive lab helps you learn about biological molecules by building molecular structures step by step.

How to Use:

  • Choose a procedure (1, 2, or 3) to begin
  • Select the correct bond shape for each molecule
  • Drag atoms from the palette to build the structure
  • Complete all molecules in each procedure to advance

Procedures:

  • Procedure 1: Simple molecules (H₂O, O₂, CH₄, CO₂)
  • Procedure 2: Amino acids and peptides
  • Procedure 3: Carbohydrates and sugars
