NAVIGATION OVERVIEW

There are 3 main sections that require interaction: the HEADER, INSTRUCTION, and WORK AREA.
Select the right arrow    to continue forward and the left arrow    to move backward
through the tutorial pages.

HEADER SECTION

The application title is shown on the left and on the right there are interactive buttons. Hover over a button
to view a description. Open a button by selecting it (for 508 use the letter). The buttons turn orange when active.
The application will dictate the interactive buttons needed, for example not all will have a video.

INSTRUCTION SECTION

This INSTRUCTION section shows a PROCEDURE, DATA, and EQUIPMENT segment. Each segment
opens like an accordion by selecting its header bar. Not all applications will require the collecting
of DATA or the use of EQUIPMENT, so these segments may look different in your application.

INSTRUCTIONS/PROCEDURES

The PROCEDURE segment contains detailed instructions for completing objectives. Switch between PROCEDURES
by selecting the numbered square, the active procedure will highlight orange. Move between pages by selecting
the right or left arrow at the bottom of the PROCEDURE column. The dots indicate the quantity of instruction pages.

INSTRUCTIONS/DATA

The DATA segment is located directly below the PROCEDURES, to open it click on the word DATA. Each DATA set will
have a number that corresponds with each PROCEDURE. Switch between DATA sets by selecting the numbered
square, it will highlight orange when it is open. Enter DATA by clicking inside one of the cells.

INSTRUCTIONS/EQUIPMENT

The EQUIPMENT segment is located directly below the DATA segment, to open it click on its header bar.
The EQUIPMENT needed is displayed with a small labeled graphic. You may see different EQUIPMENT
for each PROCEDURE. To use the EQUIPMENT, either click on it or drag-and-drop it into the work area.

WORK AREA SECTION

In this section you will utilize all items in the work area to
perform the procedures outlined.
